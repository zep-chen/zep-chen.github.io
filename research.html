Zepeng CHEN (陈泽鹏)

My research interests are in the areas of operations and marketing / finance / information system interface. Using optimization, game theory and causal inference tools, I study how individuals and firms make decisions in business area. Specifically, I am interested in studying the economic and social implications of
  • Interface between Operations and Finance: Crowdfunding, FinTech
  • Interface between Operations and Information System: Artificial Intelligence, Blockchain, Data, Learning, Open Source
  • Interface between Operations and Marketing: Behavioral pricing, Social Network
  • Interface between Operations and Learning
